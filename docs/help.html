Help Center

Get quick answers about using Job Scout Pro, applying for jobs, account access, payments, and platform support.

How do I create an account?

Go to the signup page, enter your full name, email, phone number, and password, then submit the form to register.

How do I apply for a job?

Open the jobs page, select a job you want, view the details, then click the apply button if the employer has provided an application link.

How do featured jobs work?

Featured jobs are highlighted listings that get more visibility. Employers can use the payment page to submit payment for featured placement.

Which payment methods are supported?

Job Scout Pro supports M-Pesa payments in KES and PayPal payments in USD using the official payment details.

What should I do if I cannot log in?

First check your email and password carefully. If the problem continues, contact the admin support email provided on the contact page.
